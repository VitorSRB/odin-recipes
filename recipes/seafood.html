Seafood Creole


Description

This flavorful Seafood Creole is a spicy, vibrant dish similar to a classic etouffee, featuring a rich base of tomatoes, onions, celery, and green bell peppers. By simmering a blend of firm white fish, succulent rock shrimp, and tender bay scallops in a seasoned chicken stock and tomato sauce, this recipe delivers a hearty and authentic coastal experience.

The signature of this dish is its bold heat, which comes from a potent mix of cayenne, white pepper, and hot sauce. While traditionally served "full strength" for those who love a kick, the spice level is easily adjustable to suit any palate, making it a versatile centerpiece that pairs perfectly with fluffy rice, garlic bread, and a crisp white wine.
