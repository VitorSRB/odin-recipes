World's Best Lasagna


Description

This legendary lasagna recipe has earned its reputation as one of the most popular dishes on the internet, racking up millions of views for its incredibly satisfying and filling results. While it requires a bit of extra time and effort to prepare, the combination of sweet Italian sausage, lean ground beef, and a rich triple-tomato sauce makes every minute in the kitchen worth it.

The secret to this "world's best" version is the decadent layers of ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese, perfectly bound with an egg to ensure each slice stays intact. Flavored with fresh parsley, fennel seeds, and Italian seasonings, this meaty classic creates a nostalgic, restaurant-quality meal that pairs beautifully with toasted garlic bread and a simple green salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
