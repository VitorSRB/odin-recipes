Gigi Hadid Pasta


Description

Inspired by a famous viral trend, this creamy penne dish is a family-friendly adaptation that balances rich textures with a savory tomato base. By simmering tomato paste with olive oil, garlic, and onions before adding heavy cream, the recipe creates a vibrant, velvety sauce that perfectly coats every piece of pasta.

The addition of a small amount of vodka enhances the flavors while cooking off quickly, leaving behind a sophisticated taste that appeals to both kids and adults. Finished with butter and freshly grated Parmesan cheese, this dish is a quick and comforting meal that can be easily customized with more red pepper flakes for those who prefer an extra kick of heat.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16-oz) package penne pasta
  • ½ cup pasta cooking water, or as needed
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ½ onion, diced or 1 large shallot, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste
  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons vodka
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • salt and freshly ground pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • ½ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
