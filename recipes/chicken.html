Crispy Fried Chicken


Description

This is the ultimate recipe for anyone seeking the perfect crunch. By combining a buttermilk-flour coating with a unique two-step frying method, this chicken achieves a golden, paste-like crust that locks in juices while delivering an incredibly crispy exterior.

The secret lies in starting with high heat to brown the chicken, then reducing it to cook thoroughly, and finishing with a final high-heat blast. Seasoned with paprika for a smoky flavor and beautiful browning, this dish is a proven favorite that brings the best of the Allrecipes Test Kitchen to your table.

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour for coating
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying
