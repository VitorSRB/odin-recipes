Egg Custard


Description

This classic homemade egg custard is the ultimate comfort food, cherished for its silky, spoonable texture and simple ingredient list. It is a reliable "pick-me-up" that transforms basic staples like milk, eggs, and sugar into a sophisticated dessert that can be served in individual custard cups or a single casserole bowl.

The preparation is remarkably straightforward, requiring only ten minutes of active work before baking in a gentle water bath to ensure a consistent, velvety set. Finished with a dash of vanilla and a pinch of nutmeg, this timeless recipe delivers a sweet and soothing experience that has become a go-to favorite for home cooks.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 dash vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg (Optional)
